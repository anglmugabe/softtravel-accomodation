Explore our Packages

Discover your perfect getaway with our tailored packages, whether you're seeking a serene day escape, thrilling adventures, or a relaxing retreat.

Kaya Bahari in Tofo, Inhambane Province

Welcome to our inviting A-frame beach house, where coastal charm meets comfort. Nestled right on the beach amidst swaying palm trees and the serene melody of waves, our weathered retreat offers an ideal sanctuary for those seeking a relaxed beach escape. Spread over three floors, our house not only provides ample space but also boasts stunning panoramic views of the ocean and coastline. Each level offers a unique perspective, allowing you to immerse yourself in the surrounding coastal beauty.

Bilene Chalet & Campsite Accommodation in Bilene, Gaza Province

Experience ultimate relaxation at Just In Time Resort! Our resort in the Village of Nhabanga offers wooden family chalets with indoor climate control, perfect for a home away from home. Enjoy the Coconut bar & restaurant in the beach sand, overlooking the majestic Uembje lagoon - the perfect place to experience a breathtaking sunset. Book your paradise today!

Jardim Tunduru / Tunduru Botanical Gardens in Maputo City

A budget-friendly tour package for those that would like to see the city. Behind the tall statue of Samora Machel, is a peaceful beautifully laid out botanical garden. This garden was first laid out by Thomas Honney - a well-known Englishman back in 1885.

Jardim Laulane in Mahotas, Maputo City

Afford yourself the opportunity to explore and celebrate Africa's unique heritage and discover a treasure trove of African Art. Emulsify yourself in the culture by supporting local street artists that have their art displayed.
